About the project

The Song of the Soul / La mélodie de l’âme is a bilingual reflection on the links between civilizations and the divine, from the dawn of myth to the echoes of living traditions.

The book is accompanied by a curated musical journey: each chapter resonates with one or more pieces of music, inviting the reader to explore history, myth, and inner experience through both words and sound.
